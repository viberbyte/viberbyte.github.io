frequently asked questions

How can I track my order?

As well as being notified by SMS, you can check the progress and status of your order by logging into your halal App account and clicking on "My Orders". Every product ordered will be listed, by clicking on each you will get the status of that product.

How can I change my address?

You can change you address through “My Account” > Addresses, then choose one of your addresses that you created before.

Can I donate a sacrifice or a sacrifice?

Yes, you can donate easily with the sacrifice donation feature in the Halal Mazarana application. get up by choosing the type of carcass and then choosing the donation destination you want. We will by delivering the carcass to the donor on your behalf.

I Forgot My Password, how can I retrieve it?

Tap on option “Forgot Password?” and type your number that you are used it before. Then press “send code” Insert an identification code that you received by SMS and press confirm button, enter the New password and confirm.

What payment methods are available?

You can choose one of the following payment methods (cash on delivery, credit card "Visa, MasterCard" or via Pay Link).
